Privacy Policy

Pro Calculator – Last updated: September 21, 2025

Overview

Pro Calculator is a simple, offline-first scientific calculator. We respect your privacy. This app is designed to work without collecting, storing, or sharing personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identifiers (e.g., name, email, phone number).
  • No usage analytics or crash reports.
  • No location data.
  • No contact lists, photos, media, or files.

All calculations occur on your device. Expressions and results remain in-memory. If the app offers an optional history feature, it is stored locally on your device and never transmitted by us.

Permissions

Pro Calculator generally does not request sensitive permissions. If a platform requires minimal permissions for haptics or theme settings, they are used solely to provide in-app functionality and are not used to access personal data.

Third‑Party Services

This app may include open‑source libraries (e.g., math_expressions) that run on‑device. We do not embed third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

Pro Calculator does not target children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for a child to submit to us.

Data Security

Because Pro Calculator does not transmit or store personal data on our servers, the risk of data exposure via our systems is minimized. Any data that remains on your device (e.g., calculation history) is protected by your device’s own security features.

International Users

This policy is designed to be straightforward and globally applicable. Since we do not collect personal data, obligations under regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, or LGPD are minimal. If you have any region‑specific questions, please contact us.
